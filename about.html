HISTORY

Gulf consult Architects and Engineers was established in 1980 and professionally managed organization steered under the dynamic leadership of a long experience businessman, Engr. Ali K Al-Harbi. GULF CONSULT as part of Ali K AL-Harbi group, which include AH Environmental, ETLCO and Gulf Consult Geotechnical are experienced multidisciplinary engineering consultancy firm, with more than 250 Permanent engineers in all engineering disciplines, Environmental studies, Geotechnical, Material Testing and Topographic survey services.

New Gulf Consult was founded in Cairo, Egypt in 2022 as a professional multidisciplinary engineering consultancy firm, with more than 60 Permanent engineers and as extension for Gulf Consult (A&E) in order to support the potential growth in infrastructure and building projects in KSA. New Gulf Consult in short time was able to be recognized as an important player in Infrastructure & BIM projects in Saudi Giga projects like Red Sea, Neom, King Salman Park and Diriyah Projects.
